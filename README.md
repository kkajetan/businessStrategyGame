# businessStrategyGame
The game was made in Polish. The whole idea of my creation is to get 100 000 PLN in the fastest possible time. 
We have 2 stock exchanges and a job market at our disposal.
We can look for a job, depending on our skills it will be less or better paid.
As for the exchanges, we have the cryptocurrency exchange, and the raw materials exchange. 
The rates change every turn and are random, so there is no patent that allows you to get rich on them, the only thing you can bet on is your own luck. 
Good luck have fun!
